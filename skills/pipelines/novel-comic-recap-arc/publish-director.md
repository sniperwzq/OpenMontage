# Publish Director - Novel Comic Recap Arc Pipeline

## Purpose

Package the finished arc recap for short-form platforms without spoiling protected future arcs.

## Metadata Rules

Emphasize:

- one complete mini-story,
- the betrayal/status reversal at the heart of the arc,
- the heroine's landing decision,
- next-arc curiosity.

Avoid:

- revealing protected future arcs,
- naming twists hidden by the arc brief,
- generic romance-drama titles that do not match the frame.

## Output Requirements

Create schema-valid `publish_log` with:

- final video path,
- platform title options,
- short description,
- thumbnail concept,
- hero frame recommendation,
- content warnings if needed,
- arc id and source file references in metadata.

## Title Pattern

Use direct Western short-form phrasing:

- `He Left His Luna To Die In The Snow`
- `Her Husband Chose The Other Woman First`
- `The Luna Finally Stopped Begging`

Keep titles specific to the actual arc.

## Quality Gate

- Packaging truthfully represents the completed video.
- Thumbnail concept comes from an actual hero frame.
- Metadata does not leak protected future story material.
